About Us


The Go Green Initiative works to improve lifelong outcomes for children in communities
most impacted by environmental harm by advancing environmental health, safety, and
sustainability at school. We work in partnership with district administrators, school
stakeholders, and student leaders to improve the quality of the air, water, and food at school while conserving school and community resources.
